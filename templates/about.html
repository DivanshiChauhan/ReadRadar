📖 About ReadRadar

Welcome to ReadRadar — your personalized book buddy!

This app recommends books based on your favorite reads using collaborative filtering. Whether you're into fantasy, mystery, or romance, we help you discover new titles you'll love. 💡📘

Built using Python, Flask, and data from Kaggle, it's a light and elegant tool for all book lovers. ☕✨
